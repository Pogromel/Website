What is WackingWood

During my first team project at University, we have been presented with brief to create game that will promote University of Derby. As we were able to chose team members, me and few friends chose to create game about choping wood, selling it and buying upgrades, such as fuel for chainsaw or transport for Wood Logs

This game tought me how to work with the team, how to manage time and keep deadlines everyweek for updates. It also tought me how to communicate sucesfully and how to criticies each other works in order to achieve better outcome of project.

Gameplay:

Chop the trees in procedural generated world that is endless, sell the logs, buy upgrades and become the richest lumberjack.

My Responsibilities
